Welcome to Kingdom Citizens Church International

Kingdom Citizens Church International is a vibrant family of faith, committed to raising Christians full of faith and nurturing the heirs of God’s Kingdom. We are located at Wole Iredele Street, Abiola, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria.

Our Vision

To develop Christians full of Faith.

Our Mission

To nurture the heir.

Our Mandate

To restore the lost soul and destinies back to Christ’s Kingdom for preservation through the preaching of the Word of Faith.
